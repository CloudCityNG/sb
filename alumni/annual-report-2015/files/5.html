Alumni Awards and Recognition In partnership with University Advancement, the Alumni Association celebrated the inaugural 40 Under Forty Alumni Awards on December 2, 2014 at the trendy 230 Fifth in New York City. The honorees were among Stony Brook’s brightest, most innovative, and entrepreneurial-spirited young graduates, including such notable alumni as Shutterstock Founder and CEO Jon Oringer ’96, Anthropologist and National Geographic Wildlife Correspondent Mireya Mayor ’08, and 2014 World Boxing Organization Junior Welterweight Champion Chris Algieri ’07.
